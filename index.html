Introduction

The 15th International Conference on Environmental Engineering and Applications (ICEEA 2025) will be held in Paris, France from May 27-29, 2025. The ICEEA series of conferences has been successfully held in Shanghai, Dubai, Singapore, Nottingham, Chengdu, Kuala Lumpur, Rome, Amsterdam, Prague, and Madrid. ICEEA 2025 aims to provide a forum for researchers and practitioners in the field to share ideas, designs, and experiments. We welcome participation from academia, industry, and government agencies.

Dates and Location

The conference will be held from May 27th to29th, 2025 at the Paris Convention Centre, Paris, France.

Conference Schedule
