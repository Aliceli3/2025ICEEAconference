Introduction

The 15th International Conference on Environmental Engineering and Applications (ICEEA 2025) will be held in Paris, France from May 27-29, 2025. The ICEEA series of conferences has been successfully held in Shanghai, Dubai, Singapore, Nottingham, Chengdu, Kuala Lumpur, Rome, Amsterdam, Prague, and Madrid. ICEEA 2025 aims to provide a forum for researchers and practitioners in the field to share ideas, designs, and experiments. We welcome participation from academia, industry, and government agencies.

Dates and Location

The conference will be held from May 27th to29th, 2025 at the Paris Convention Centre, Paris, France.

Call for Papers

2025 15th International Conference on Environmental Engineering and Applications (ICEEA 2025) is the premier forum for the presentation of new advances and research results in the fields of theoretical, experimental, and applied Environmental Engineering and Applications. The conference will bring together leading researchers, engineers and scientists in the domain of interest from around the world. Topics of interest for submission include, but are not limited to:

Ecosystem management and sustainable development

  • Global environmental change and ecosystem management
  • Integrated ecosystem management
  • Environmental restoration and ecological engineering

Environmental monitoring and management

  • Hazardous substances and detection techniques
  • Biodegradation of harmful substances
  • Monitoring and analysis of environmental pollutants

Water resources management and water pollution control

  • Water resources and watershed management
  • Industrial wastewater treatment
  • Wastewater and sludge treatment

Atmospheric science and air pollution control

  • Atmospheric physics
  • Climate and Climate change
  • Atmospheric modeling and numerical values
